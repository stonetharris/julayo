Julayo Project 2023

Everyone deserves access to equitable healthcare.

Our mission is to build healthcare facilities in underserved areas which provide essential services and employment opportunities. Our first project location is Akure, Nigeria.

Akure, Nigeria has 400,000 inhabitants and only one public hospital. In Akure, people suffer from preventable and easily treatable diseases.
